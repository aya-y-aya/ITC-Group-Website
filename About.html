What you need to know about HoteLuna

HoteLuna welcomes you into a real cosmopolitan and vibrant environment while still providing solitude and intimacy. Exceptional fusion of spectacular architecture and divine cuisine, in perfect harmony; timeless elegance adapted to the needs of our time. HoteLuna was established on November 2022 at Bonifacio Global City. Founded by HoteLuna Co. on September 14, 2022. The hotel's mission is "to provide exemplary, personalized service that goes above and beyond expectations." Our Vision is "to uphold the reputation we have with customers, partners, competitors, and the larger community by continuing to implement and set the highest standards of service quality."
